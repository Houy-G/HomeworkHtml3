Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer catering services?

Yes, we offer catering for events of all sizes. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance to discuss your needs.

Can I book the space for a private event?

Yes, our space is available for private events during and after business hours. Please contact us for availability and pricing.

Do you have Wi-Fi?

Yes, we offer free Wi-Fi for all customers. Ask our staff for the current password.

Are dogs allowed?

Well-behaved dogs are welcome in our outdoor seating area. Service animals are welcome throughout our establishment.
